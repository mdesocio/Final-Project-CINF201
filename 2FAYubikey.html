What is Yubikey and How do I Use One?

Yubikey is a hardware usb key that is often used as a physical Two Factor Device for accessing accounts using FIDO or U2F technologies. Users add these to their accounts like their Google Account, Microsoft Account, etc. These devices are far less recognized and used on accounts. Often companies don't implement the ability to use these devices. Slack, OVH, and many game developer companies also don't allow for the use of U2F or FIDO keys.
So how does one setup a security key. Provided is the steps on how to deploy your new U2F Yubikey on your Google Account.

  1. Open your Google Account by going to https://myaccount.google.com.
  2. Navigate to "Security" on the left side.
  3. In the Security menu, go to the "Sign in to Google">"2-Step Verification"
  4. Turn on "2-Step Verification"
  5. Click on "Add a Security"
  6. Follow the prompts of Google
